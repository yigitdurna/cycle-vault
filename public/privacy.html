cycle vault

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

The short version: cycle vault has no accounts, no servers, and no analytics. Everything you enter stays on your device. We — the developer — never receive, store, see, sell, or share your data, because there is no system on our side that could. We literally cannot read it.

1. Who this applies to

This policy describes how the cycle vault application ("the app", "we") handles your information. It applies to the iOS app and the web version at yigitdurna.github.io/cycle-vault.

2. What the app stores

The app lets you record information about your menstrual cycle. Everything you choose to log is stored only on your own device. This can include:

  • Period and cycle dates (start and end dates)
  • Symptom logs: mood, energy, cramps, sleep, flow, pain (including location and severity), and whether a day affected your activities
  • Free-text notes you write
  • Your settings, such as a custom average cycle length

On the web version this is held in your browser's local storage (under keys such as cycle-tracker-calendar-v4, cycle-tracker-daylogs-v1 and cycle-tracker-settings-v1). On iOS it is held in the app's local on-device storage.

3. What the app does NOT do

  • It does not require an account, email address, phone number, or login of any kind.
  • It does not send your cycle or symptom data to us or to any server. There is no backend.
  • It does not include advertising, third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or marketing SDKs.
  • It does not ask for your name, location, or the region/state you live in.
  • It does not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone — there is nothing on our side to sell.

4. Data you choose to export

The app can export your data to a JSON or CSV file when you ask it to, from the Settings screen. That file is created on your device and saved wherever you choose. Once you share or move that file, it is outside the app's control and subject to the privacy of wherever you send it. We never see it.

5. Device backups

Because your data lives on your device, it may be included in backups that you control — for example an encrypted iPhone or iCloud backup, or your browser's profile. Those backups are governed by Apple's or your browser vendor's terms, not by us. If the iOS app offers optional iCloud sync, that data is end-to-end encrypted by Apple and remains unreadable to us.

6. Your control over your data

  • Export a full copy at any time (JSON or CSV) from Settings.
  • Delete everything at any time using "Clear all data" in Settings.
  • Uninstalling the app, or clearing the website's storage, permanently removes the data from that device.

7. What local-only privacy does and does not protect

We believe in being precise rather than overstating. Keeping data on your device means there is no central database for us to be breached, subpoenaed, or pressured to hand over — we hold nothing. However, local storage does not protect data on a device that someone else can unlock and open. For sensitive situations we strongly recommend enabling your device passcode and biometric lock (and the app's lock feature, where available). Because we never hold your data, we also cannot recover it for you if your device is lost.

8. Children

The app is a personal tracking tool and is not directed at children under 13. It collects no information from anyone, including children, on our servers — because it has none.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new version will be published at this address. Material changes will be reflected in the app where appropriate.
